Ergonomic Chair

Chair with adjustable height, width, and angles. Made of soft materials, and curves suitable for human bodies. Ergonomic chair can protect coders' backs and spines and prevent some potential career-diseases.

Vertical Monitor

Buy a normal monitor, then flip it 90 degree. A vertical monitor provides coder with a wider view of the code. It's also good to have another horizontal monitor on the side, providing a full view of the code-preview.

Mechanical Keyboard

Unlike laptop keyboards, mechanical keyboards provides firmer and sharp clicks and reactions when typing. They give the programmer more control over thier codes and the clicking sounds make them relaxed.

Ultrawide Monitor

Unlike normal monitors with 16:9 aspect ratio, ultrawide monitors have 21:9, or even 32:9 aspect ratio, giving filmmakers and edittors the ability to preview the video while adjusting video properties using software's side panel. Films with 21:9 aspect ratio also looks greater on this monitor.

Control Pad

Editting softwares requires a lot of uses of, and combinations of function keys, those hard-to-remember shortcuts promote the need for the control pad , where the shortcuts are packaged as individual keys.

Storage Station

Raw footages are extremely large and impossible to be stored without the help of external drives. This storage station is used for this. It also provides external software protections over the footages.
