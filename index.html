FOR PROGRAMMERS

Programmers are sedentary workers.

It's important for them to have a comfortable chair, and a good keyboard.

Additional monitors will also do them favors, vertical monitor will make it easy to read codes.


FOR FILMMAKERS

Filmmakers need more real estate and larger storage.

Ultrawide monitor will help them better work in editing softwares.

A set of control pads help them navigate through editing softwares more efficiently.
